Food Writer · Cookbook Author

Harris Salat

Cooking with people, writing down what they know, and passing it on.

I'm a James Beard Award–nominated food writer. I've written about food and culture for The New York Times and Saveur, and I'm the co-author, with chef Tadashi Ono, of Japanese Soul Cooking, Japanese Hot Pots, and the new Japanese Comfort Cooking — plus Takashi's Noodles with Takashi Yagihashi.

Alongside the books, I make free cook booklets. Each one starts the same way: in a kitchen with a great home cook, recording as they work, then researching the cuisine's traditions to test, cross-check, and fill in what the transcripts leave out. Three of them live on this site — help yourself.
